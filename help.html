Frequently Asked Questions

To join a club, navigate to the Clubs page, find the club you're interested in, and click the "Join Club" button. You'll need to be logged in to complete this action.

Visit the Events page, select the event you want to attend, and click the "Register" button. You'll receive a confirmation email with event details.

Joining clubs offers networking opportunities, skill development, leadership experience, and access to exclusive events and workshops.
