About PCE Connect

PCE Connect is your gateway to a vibrant campus life at Pillai College of Engineering. We bring together students, clubs, and events on one unified platform.

Our Mission

To create a seamless digital ecosystem that empowers students to engage in campus activities, develop leadership skills, and build lasting connections within the PCE community.

  • Foster student engagement
  • Streamline club management
  • Facilitate event organization

Our Vision

To become the central hub for student activities and club management, creating a more connected and engaged campus community at PCE.

  • Innovation in student engagement
  • Digital transformation of campus life
  • Building tomorrow's leaders

What We Offer

Club Management

Efficiently manage club activities, members, and events through our integrated platform.

Event Organization

Create, promote, and manage events with ease. Track registrations and attendance.

Analytics & Insights

Get valuable insights into club performance and event success metrics.
